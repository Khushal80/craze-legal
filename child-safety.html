Craze Child Safety Standards

Craze is committed to providing a safe environment for all users and has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitments

  • We prohibit any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Users may report inappropriate content or accounts through the app.
  • Reported content is reviewed and removed when it violates our policies.
  • Accounts engaging in illegal activity are permanently suspended.
  • We cooperate with applicable law enforcement authorities where legally required.
